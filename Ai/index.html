How The Process Works

You Share
Team Analyzes
Teams Coordinate
We Protect

1. You Share Information

If you see something strange or a potential weakness in an AI system, you report it. It doesn't have to be a confirmed attackâ€”even suspicious activity is helpful. The earlier, the better!

2. The Team Analyzes It

Experts at Meraki Amods 6966 take the information you shared, combine it with other reports, and figure out what's going on. They work to understand the threat and how serious it might be.

3. Teams Coordinate

The security team works with other internal groups and partners. They collaborate on the best way to handle the threat and prepare a unified defense.

4. We Protect Our Systems

The final step is taking action. This could mean sharing detection tools, notifying affected users, and distributing alerts to protect our infrastructure from the AI threat.
